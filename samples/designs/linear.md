# Design System Inspired by Linear

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Linear's website is a masterclass in dark-mode-first product design — a near-black canvas (`#08090a`) where content emerges from darkness like starlight.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary
- **Deep Space** (`#08090a`): The near-black page background.
- **Linear Purple** (`#5e6ad2`): The single signature brand accent used for CTAs and interactive moments.

### Surface & Background
- **Page Background** (`#08090a`): The primary canvas.
- **Card Surface** (`#0f1011`): Barely-elevated panels.
- **Warm Sand** (`#1a1b1e`): Subtle container backgrounds.

### Neutrals & Text
- **Primary Text** (`#f7f8f8`): Soft, luminous body and heading text.
- **Secondary Text** (`#8a8f98`): Muted metadata and captions.
- **Tertiary Text** (`#62666d`): De-emphasized footnotes.

### Semantic & Accent
- **Border Soft** (`#1e2024`): Barely-visible separators.
- **Border Warm** (`#2a2c31`): Prominent dividers.
- **Error** (`#eb5757`): Red for warnings.
- **Focus Ring** (`#5e6ad2`): Keyboard focus indicator.

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Headline**: `Inter Variable`, with fallback: `Inter`
- **Body / UI**: `Inter Variable`, with fallback: `Inter`
- **Code**: `Berkeley Mono`, with fallback: `JetBrains Mono`
